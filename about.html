Our Story

Paint and Board Boutique was established in November 2019. Owner and designer Kelly Brannon created this unique space to share her passions with her community as a collaborative designer, artist, and teacher. PBB Studio offers a place to express your creativity with the company of other customers, family, and friends through workshops, paint in place studio hours, and private parties.

Paint & Boards Boutique celebrates creativity and style combining a passion for design, woodworking, paint, furniture and home décor. Retailing Jolie Home matte paint products, Prima Redesign, Dixie Belle, Mudd Pie, Blue Ocean Trader, to provide customers with an array of gifts, home décor, up-cycled, recycled and handmade treasures.

Paint & Boards Boutique also ​offers a beautiful range of hand painted, refinished furniture. We are also available to do custom paint projects for customers in any style and will happily assist you in choosing colors and designs.

The Studio offers a space to explore your creative side, play with color, explore finishes and textures. June will kick off a host of workshops, classes and private sessions. Stay tuned for more information on private gatherings, parties and summer camps.

Furniture restoration, custom painting, kitchen and bath cabinet reface and paint services also available.

Paint & Boards Boutique is open for business. Please stop by and support our new business venture! We look forward to an official grand opening as soon as we can!
